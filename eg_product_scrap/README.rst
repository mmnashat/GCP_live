=================================
V13 Product Low Stock Notification
=================================

Maintaining inventory is a tedious task and it becomes more difficult when we have a lot of different products. This app sends an email to the specified users when the stock goes below-defined qty. So that the purchase manager or inventory manager gets to know about that and can take further steps to maintain the required qty.

=======================================
This app used to send an notification email to users for list of products whose quantity is lower than Minimum Quantity.

And also allow to Generate the Excel Report for a list of products whose quantity is lower than Minimum Quantity at (Global & Individual) level.
